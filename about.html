Our history

Our family history

Shi Yao Ming and his parents immigrated to China in 1980 from Indonesia. He grew up in Hongkong and attended Xiamen University as a Culinary major. Following graduation, he found his true calling in the culinary field. Bucking cultural expectations, he started his culinary career in Hongkong. From there, he worked in Taiwan, Beijing and Guangzhou at restaurants Jardiniere and Union Pacific. After more than 20 years of dedication and mastering the complexities of the Culinary industry, he has decided to pursue his dream and follow his cultural roots. This dream is to reimagine and share the cuisine of his heritage in his next culinary adventure, 食香园 Restaurant.
